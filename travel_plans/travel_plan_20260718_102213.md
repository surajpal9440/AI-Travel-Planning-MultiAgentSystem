* Cost: Accommodation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000), food and transportation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000)

**Day 9-11: Kanazawa**

* Visit popular attractions like the Kenrokuen Garden, Kanazawa Castle, and the Higashi Chaya District.
* Cost: Accommodation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000), food and transportation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000)

**Day 12-15: Tokyo**

* Take a train to Tokyo and stay at a budget-friendly hostel or guesthouse, such as the Tokyo Hana Hostel or the Guesthouse Shinjuku.
* Visit popular attractions like the Tokyo Tower, Meiji Shrine, and the Shibuya Crossing.
* Cost: Accommodation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000), food and transportation (approximately ₹5,000 - ₹7,000)

* Food and transportation: ₹20,000 - ₹30,000

**Tips:**

* Book flights and accommodation in advance to get the best prices.
