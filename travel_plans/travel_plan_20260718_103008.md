### Day 1-2: Pune to Osaka, Japan
- **Travel Dates:** Book flights from Pune or Mumbai to Osaka (as it's often cheaper than flying directly to Tokyo). Look for affordable options like Air India, Japan Airlines, or other carriers that might offer better deals.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in a hostel or a budget hotel in Osaka for the first two days to get accustomed to the city and its surroundings.
- **Activities:** Explore Osaka Castle, Dotonbori, and try the local street food.

### Day 3-5: Osaka
- **Activities:** Visit the Universal Studios Japan, Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan, and take a stroll through the Shinsekai district.
- **Transportation:** Use the Osaka Metro for easy travel within the city.

### Day 6-8: Kyoto
- **Travel to Kyoto:** Take a train ( JR Kyoto Line) from Osaka to Kyoto. The journey is about 1 hour and costs around ¥560.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in a budget-friendly hostel or guesthouse.
- **Activities:** Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji Temple, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

### Day 9-11: Kanazawa
- **Travel to Kanazawa:** Take the train (JR Hokuriku Shinkansen) from Kyoto to Kanazawa, which takes about 2.5 hours and costs approximately ¥6,500. However, to save money, consider using a bus or a slower train.
- **Accommodation:** Find a budget hotel or hostel.
- **Activities:** Explore the Kenrokuen Garden, Kanazawa Castle, and the Higashi Chaya District.

### Day 12-15: Tokyo
- **Travel to Tokyo:** Take the train (JR Hokuriku Shinkansen and then JR Tokaido Shinkansen) from Kanazawa to Tokyo, which takes about 4 hours and costs around ¥14,000. For a cheaper option, consider an overnight bus.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in a budget hostel or guesthouse in Tokyo.
- **Activities:** Visit the Tokyo Tower, Meiji Shrine, Shibuya Crossing, and explore the Asakusa district.
